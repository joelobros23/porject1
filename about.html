Mang Knorr is a delightful restaurant located in the heart of Bacolod City, Negros Occidental. We are proud to offer an authentic and satisfying dining experience that captures the essence of Filipino cuisine. Our menu features a wide selection of classic Filipino dishes, as well as a variety of innovative twists on traditional favorites. At Mang Knorr, we believe that great food starts with the freshest ingredients, and we source our produce from local farms and markets to ensure that every dish is bursting with flavor. Our team of experienced chefs takes great care in preparing each dish to perfection, using only the finest quality ingredients and traditional cooking techniques. Whether you are in the mood for a hearty meal with friends and family or a quick bite on-the-go, Mang Knorr has something for everyone. Come visit us today and discover why we are one of the most beloved restaurants in Bacolod City!
